About TurcExport

Serving in the field of foreign trade and consultancy, TurcExport has an important position among the leading companies in trade breakthroughs in Turkey and in the world.

Thanks to our strong connections with Turkey's leading industrial organizations and our experience of more than 40 years in the chemical industry, we are at foreign trade events with our stakeholders in order to deliver Turkey's domestic and national resources to our connections abroad, and to serve with our wide range of products from raw materials to fast moving consumer goods.

With our expert staff, experience and technical know-how, we are the address of TRUST in exports to serve our valued customers, in the best possible way.
